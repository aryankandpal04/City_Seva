About CitySeva

Our Mission

CitySeva is dedicated to bridging the gap between citizens and local government, making civic engagement more accessible and efficient.

What We Do

CitySeva provides a comprehensive platform for citizens to:

  • Submit and track civic complaints
  • Communicate directly with government officials
  • Monitor the status of their concerns
  • Provide feedback on service delivery
